Peanut Butter Toast

Peanut Butter Toast like the 2 previous foods is a classic and is what I eat mostly everyday for breakfast. It combines flavor, protein, and grains to deliver a nutritious and filling breakfast and is what I eat most.

Ingredients

  • Loaf of Bread
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Slice the bread in half
  2. Put both slices in the toaster for however you would like the bread to be cooked
  3. Get your peanut butter out and spread peanut butter evenly on both sides
  4. Put the slices of bread on top of each other evenly
