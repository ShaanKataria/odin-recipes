Cereal

Cereal is a classic breakfast eaten by many. I myself love to enjoy a bowl of cereal before kreafast. I call it the perfect balance breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Cheerios
  • Milk
  • Bowl

Steps

  1. Get out your bowl
  2. Open the cheerios box and pour a decent amount of cheerios into the bowl
  3. Add a good amount of milk to the Bowl
