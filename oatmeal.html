Oatmeal

Like cereal, oatmeal is also a classic and original breakfast that people eat every day. The thing about it is, it is sweet and steaming hot which will make you feel good of course. I personally love it.

Ingredients

  • Quaker Oats Packets
  • Bowl

Steps

  1. Open the packet and dump it into the bowl
  2. Fill water up to the thick line in the empty packet
  3. Pour that water into the bowl
  4. Put the bowl into the microwave for 1 and a half to 2 minutes
